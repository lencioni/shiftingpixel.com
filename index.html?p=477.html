Some old boardgames at the Flea Market and Antique Show. I had actually never heard or seen any of these before.. but Lotto, Colorforms, Popeye’s Birthday Party, and MAD Magazine Game all sound like they could have been fun. I bet most of the pieces were missing.

I went up to Duluth this weekend–it was very nice up there. Sam and I went down by the lake and sat on some of the rocks and the weather was just perfect. I wish I lived in a city where you don’t need air conditioning to be comfortable.
