Black Dog, Red Door by Joe Lencioni

December 9, 2007 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/1.4, 1/90 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

This dog was super nice, but incredibly camera shy.

I’ve never seen anything quite like it before–I tried to get a good photo but every time I pointed my camera at her she curled into a little ball and hid. I did, however, manage to get this shot that I liked.
