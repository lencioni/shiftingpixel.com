My Ikea lamp by Joe Lencioni

April 27, 2006 by Joe Lencioni

Just a simple diptych of my sweet Japanese-style lamp from Ikea that hangs in the corner of my living room just behind my computer. It’s one of those lamps that has a bunch of boxes made out of paper that hang on a line from the ceiling. I think it’s pretty sweet. If you’re interested in buying it, I don’t think they sell it anymore but this one is close.

And, if you’re a Holga fan, don’t worry… I have a couple more from my last roll that I will put up soon enough.
