Cabinets two and three by Joe Lencioni

December 15, 2005 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/3.5, 1/2 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

Some cabinets belonging to the Gustavus Choir. I saw these in the music building when I was on the way to my office one day and just had to come back later to shoot them. I have a few more from the music building at Gustavus Adolphus College that also turned out well. I have a couple more shots of the bosc pear, but I was boring myself so I decided I better switch it up a little bit.

Tasted the homebrew tonight. It was quite excellent, but a little bit on the strong tasting side. Sam tried it and didn’t really like it too much because it was so strong and thick… But I like the thickness. Still, it’s not quite as thick as Guinness. If you are in the area, let me know and we’ll have some beers sometime.
