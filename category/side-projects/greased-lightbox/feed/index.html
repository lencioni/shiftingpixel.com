]]> http://shiftingpixel.com/2006/07/21/greased-lightbox-v014/feed/ 12 Greased Lightbox v0.13 http://shiftingpixel.com/2006/06/07/greased-lightbox-v013/ http://shiftingpixel.com/2006/06/07/greased-lightbox-v013/#comments Thu, 08 Jun 2006 01:50:56 +0000 Joe Lencioni http://shiftingpixel.com/2006/06/07/greased-lightbox-v013/ I just finished up Greased Lightbox v0.13.

While this is not really a huge update, there are a couple of cool new things.

First, images that are larger than your window will automatically be resized to the window size because it makes life happier and hopefully this will save you a little bit of time. If you want to see the image at its original size, press the ’0′ key. I have been meaning to add this for a while but haven’t gotten around to it until now. I’d like to hear what you think of this.

Also, due to request, I added support for images hosted with the image hosting site, Image Socket. I hope you will enjoy that.

Additionally, I added a new Czech translation sent in by Hejda. If you know another language that is not covered yet (or just partially covered), please check out the code toward the bottom of the script and send me your translation.

Finally, just a side note, my stats tell me that Greased Lightbox has been downloaded over 21,500 times. I’m really glad that people seem to be enjoying it. If you like it, please spread the word by blogging about it or passing a link onto a friend or two.

Change Log

  • Automatically resizes images that are larger than window
  • Image Socket support
  • New Czech translation (thanks Hejda!)
  • Fixed context link on error message
  • Minor bugs squashed
