Arrow Ace Hardware by Joe Lencioni

November 7, 2005 by Joe Lencioni

Similar photos

I don’t know why, but I have always loved giant murals. I think that they are so interesting and add a lot of character to a plain brick wall. And, while this is a little bit less of a mural and a bit more of a billboard, I still like it for some reason. It is on the wall of the Arrow Ace hardware store in town. I can just imagine how it will look in forty years if they don’t keep it up. It’ll be all faded and the paint will be peeling and stuff. That’ll be totally cool.
