Nominations are currently open for the 2007 Photobloggies, but they close in only three more days!

If you are a photoblog reader (which you are if you are reading this), you really need to head over to the Photobloggies site and nominate your favorite photoblogs in all of the appropriate categories.

Which categories is Shifting Pixel eligible for?

If you’d like to nominate Shifting Pixel, that would be really cool. Here are the categories in which I am primarily eligible:

  • Best American Photoblog
  • Best Writing
  • Best Kept Secret
  • Best Photoblog Design
  • Photo of the Year
  • Photoblog of the Year

There are also a smattering of additional topical categories that I may or may not entirely fit into (Best Black and White Photography, Best Animal Photography, Best Abstract Photography, Best Landscape Photography, and Best Toy Camera Photography). I’ll let you be the judge on that one.

I’m looking forward to seeing this year’s results, so go nominate your favorites!
