Another one from the Mark Mallman show.

I started using a new search engine today called Blingo. It is fast and uses Google for its search results, so they are good. The twist is that every time you search, you have a chance to win a prize like an iPod or a PSP. It doesn’t cost anything and I havent received any spam because of it yet. Plus, it is a fairly attractive yet not really all that in your face search engine. Their theme is speech bubbles. Give Blingo a spin.

And have you heard of Smartcar yet? If you got a few G’s to blow, I’d like one for Christmas. Unfortunately they are not exactly available in the US yet. I’ll be saving my pennies for when they get here. If you live in a country where they have these things and have actually gotten to take a ride in one (or even better yet you own one!), let me know what you thought.
