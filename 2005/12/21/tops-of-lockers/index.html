Tops of Lockers by Joe Lencioni

December 21, 2005 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/5.6, 2 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

The tops of some lockers in the music building at Gustavus Adolphus College. I was a little hesitant to put this one up because I just don’t think it’s all that great. Maybe it is and I have a skewed opinion because I’ve been staring at a computer screen constantly for the past few days and have been putting in some long hours at the office to get some end of the year projects wrapped up… But, since I am short on time right now here it is. Enjoy.

With that said, I have a sooper dooper busy week ahead of me so posting may end up being sporadic at best. Hang in there kids, the new year is almost here.

Nerd news: I was feeling wacky, so I added some XMP metadata readout info to each image’s page. It’s not exactly how I want it yet, so I’ll be tweaking it some more later on. But I figured that it was cool enough to put up right away. I hope you like the new extra info.

Photo of the day – I miss canoeing up north. It’s so peaceful, it’s like medicine.
