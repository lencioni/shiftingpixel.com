Triangle torso by Joe Lencioni

October 2, 2005 by Joe Lencioni

Similar photos

I thought this was so weird. It is a sign on a table at the Rock Bend Folk Festival that had a lot of information about one of the wetlands in the area (I didn’t take too much time to read it, but I think it was about Hallet’s Pond in St. Peter). Underneath the sign was this mannequin torso with a wig and a top and everything, just chillin there. I couldn’t really figure out why. It’s not really the greatest photo, but it is so weird that I just had to post it.
