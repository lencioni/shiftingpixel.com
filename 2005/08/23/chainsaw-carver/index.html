Chainsaw carver by Joe Lencioni

August 23, 2005 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/5.6, 1/1500 s, 400 ISO

Similar photos

One of those chainsaw carver men at the Nicollet County Fair. He had a few pretty cool carvings around him that he had done. I think he was working on a wolf or something. There was a mini minigolf course at the fair that you can see behind the carver in the background.

I was away for the weekend up in Crosslake, MN so I left the photoblog on autopilot. Thanks everybody for the nice comments. I went out taking some night shots with Sam but most of them didn’t turn out. I do, however, think that I have one that is decent that I’ll post after all of the county fair shots are through.
