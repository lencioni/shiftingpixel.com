The second in the series. I really like the two shapes in the middle of the frame because they look so much like faces. I was just amazed when I was looking at these for the first time because so many of them have faces in them, but I guess that our brains are trained to see faces so it might just be psychological. These shots were taken just north of the EconoFoods grocery store and the car dealer in town.
