August 11, 2005 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/5.6, 1/2 s, 100 ISO

Fifth in the series. I think this might be my least favorite of the series, but I wanted to post it anyways because I really like how it seems to convey movement of some of the light balls. It looks as though they are dancing and playing with each other above a calm pond. I know… I’m lame. Get over it.

Well, tomorrow we should be back to some more normal things I think, but I’ll have to see what I have come up with first. Unless you really like these because I have some more that I can post if you’re not sick of them.
