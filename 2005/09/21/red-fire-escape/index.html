Lamp post against a building

Red fire escape by Joe Lencioni

September 21, 2005 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/8.0, 1/125 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

Some building with a red fire escape and some birds on it that I saw while I was in Montreal this summer. I remember walking past this and seeing a ton of birds perched on the edge of the roof. I was trying to get a better shot of all the birds, but I was just too slow getting my camera out and stuff. Still, I dig the shadow cast by the stairs. However, I think that I really like this shot mostly because of the two birds in mid flight. It’s a zen thing, like how many babies fit in a tire.
