One of the concession stands at the Rock Bend Folk Festival a couple of weeks ago here in St. Peter. This is definitely not the greatest photo, but it is from my first roll of film I ever shot in a Holga camera. Things that I learned from doing this roll: Holga is fun, 120 film is cool, scanning film sucks especially when there is a lot of dust on the negatives. I think there are a few more on this roll that I will end up using.

Also, I tried for so long to get the right EXIF data into the file and downloaded exifer and stuff, but nothing seemed to let me edit the things that I wated to, like camera information. If you know how to do that, please let me know.

Today I am going to the Sigur Ros concert at the Orpheum Theatre in Minneapolis. I am very excited to see these guys… I don’t think they have been in Minnesota in years and it is going to be a very great time and a very great show. If you haven’t heard their stuff before, I would highly recommend their () album. Their other stuff is great too, but I especially like (). However, they just released a new album recently called Takk… They are from Iceland. Best band ever.

Update (09/26/2005): Oops… The Sigur Ros show was at the State theatre, which is just down the block from the Orpheum. They are quite similar though, so it’s easy to see how one might get them confused.
