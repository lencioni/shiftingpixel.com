Ring of Fire revisited by Joe Lencioni

August 16, 2006 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/2.8, 1/1500 s, 400 ISO

Similar photos

Last year’s shot of the Ring of Fire ride, revisited.

This year’s is focused in on the ride operator and riders right at the moment when the cars are suspended upside down at the top of the ring. The operator stares at the riders as they hang upside down. His hand gently teases the go lever.
