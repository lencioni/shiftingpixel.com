I went for a walk in the Linneaus Arboretum at Gustavus Adolphus College today and it was exactly what I needed. The weather was so absolutely nice and I had the time to take a few photos.

I can’t remember the last time that I spent a such good chunk of time outside. I can’t believe how much I need time like that. It makes me want to go camping.
