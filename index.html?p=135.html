My father by Joe Lencioni

October 6, 2005 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/4.0, 1/20 s, 3200 ISO

Similar photos

This is my dad. His name is Joe, just like me. However, our middle names are different, so that means I am not a Jr. or a II or anything like that. I took this photo right when I got in the doorway of my parents’ house for Thanksgiving in 2003. So, it’s a bit of an older shot from the archives. I was going to try and post something new so I went out shooting last night and got absolutely nothing worthwhile. But that’s okay because I really like how this photo turned out. I also cropped quite a bit of it off the top to give it a panorama-esque feel, which is something that I usually haven’t done.
