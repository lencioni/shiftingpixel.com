Sleeveless in Montreal by Joe Lencioni

September 23, 2005 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/5.6, 1/90 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

Lame title. I suck at titles.

The Just For Laughs festival was going on while I was in Montreal this summer and it was great. There were tons of interesting people everywhere and shows going on all to have a little fun. This picture features some of the strangest and funniest costumes that I saw at the festival. It’s just some heads with legs running around the streets and pestering people with their large tongues sticking out. The guy in the front without the sleeves was just some random dude walking down the sidewalk.

I almost went black and white on this photo, but I really liked the colors in the man’s shirt and how the cute, little, pink heart sticker stood out in color. The guy has a unique look going for him, but I think he makes it work.
