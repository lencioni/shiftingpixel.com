If you want to win a free 8″ x 10″ print (or 10″ x 10″ for square prints) of your choice from shifting pixel, read on.

I have decided that it would be a lot of fun to hold a contest here. The rules are simple and the prize is cool, so be sure to participate if you can.

How to win

  1. Put a link to shifting pixel on your website or add it to your blogroll.
  2. Leave a comment here and be sure to use your real e-mail address and a link to your site. Also, if you’d like to, include a link to the photo you would like to win. (If you are already linking here, that’s cool too, just let me know.)
  3. ???
  4. Profit.

Deadline

The deadline for entries is Saturday, October 14 at 5 p.m. Central time. I will draw a winner shortly after.

The Fine Print

Not all photos on shifting pixel are available as prints. Some exclusions apply.

Start Linking!

Go ahead and start linking. And, to make things interesting, if there are enough entries I might draw two winners.
