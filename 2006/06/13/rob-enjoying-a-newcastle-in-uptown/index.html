Rob George, enjoying a humongous, delicious Newcastle brown ale at a bar in Uptown at his bachelor party. Since I’m not really familiar with the area, I don’t remember what the place is called, but I think it might be William’s. In any case, they have 70 different beers on tap and over 300 in bottles. It’s pretty amazing. I didn’t get any photos of their massive lineup of taps, though, because it was pretty busy when we were there.
