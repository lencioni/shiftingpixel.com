Church Window by Joe Lencioni

November 19, 2006 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/8.0, 370 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

This photo of the big window on the south side of the building is probably my favorite from the Norseland Lutheran Church shoot.

Went out last night with Jerry, Andrew, Molly, and Ryan to the Kasota Prairie to try and shoot the Leonids. It was freezing cold and I didn’t have much luck capturing the event, but I’m not sure about the other guys. I did get a cool shot or two of the night sky with some wicked looking trees in the foreground that I’ll post soon.
