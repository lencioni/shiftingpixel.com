Amur Tiger – Panthera tigris altaica by Joe Lencioni

November 27, 2006 by Joe Lencioni
Minnesota Zoo ƒ/1.4, 1/250 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

This tiger is also known as the Siberian, Korean, Manchurian, or North China Tiger and is currently classified as a critically endangered species.

The Tiger Lair on the Northern Trail at the Minnesota Zoo was my favorite part of the whole zoo because these two Amur Tigers came right up to the glass. I was crouched down for a photo and one of the tigers jumped up right in front of me and put its paws on the glass. It was quite intimidating. I waited around for a while and they started fighting… They were just playing, but it was really cool to watch.
