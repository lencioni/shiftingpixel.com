Behind the Swanson Tennis Center there were some smaller auxillery buildings that looked like they were probably not much more than humongous air pumps. They all had big vents like this one on the sides of them, but this one was damaged and had a big slash through the middle of it.

Olympics

Have you been watching the Olympics at all? It would be so cool to be there and see the games in person. I would love to go to Italy in general. Sam and I watched some of the games tonight because some kid, Mason Aguirre, who she used to teach confirmation to a few years ago is in the half-pipe snowboarding competition. He did quite well when we were watching, so that was pretty cool and exciting. Although, I think my favorite events are figure skating, bobsled, and luge…

Full Moon

Full moon tomorrow. There is a fair chance that I will be able to get out and take some sweet night shots, but no promises… especially if it is as cold as it was today. Plus, I will be having some company over, so anything could happen really.
