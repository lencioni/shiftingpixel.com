The busted up truck from the Nicollet County Fair again. Out of the series so far, this one is definitely my least favorite. I almost didn’t put it in at all, but I thought it was pretty good. It has a lot of nice angles and geometry things going on. Also, I think that it shows the destruction of the car the best of the series.
