From a historic Methodist church in Cleveland, Minnesota. Although this one is mostly not in focus, I’m really pleased with how it turned out. I am especially fond of the shadows and the geometry.

Things are finally starting to warm up for real around here and I’m getting excited for the next full moon. It will be just the right temperature for taking night shots… Hopefully it won’t be too muddy, too.
