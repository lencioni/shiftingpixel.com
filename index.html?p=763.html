Lake Superior, just north of Croftville, MN ƒ/2.5, 1/80 s, 400 ISO

That little, white dot up there is the almost full moon. It was really bright just as the sun was going down.

I took this panorama in the same spot as these two other photos.

This is a composite of twelve individual photographs. Unfortunately, since I don’t have much experience with taking panoramas, it didn’t turn out quite as well as I had hoped it would, but that’s okay with me. I thought it was still worth posting.
