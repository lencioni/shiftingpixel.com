Nobel Hall of Science by Joe Lencioni

May 3, 2008 by Joe Lencioni
Gustavus Adolphus College, Saint Peter, Minnesota ƒ/5.6, 1/640 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

This is where the science happens at Gustavus. You are looking at the main entrance, and definitely the most interesting part of the building.

Out of the last few Gustavus building photos I’ve posted, I like this one the most. I’m not entirely sure why, but I think it has to do with all of the interesting geometry going on throughout the frame. And, just like the previous two photos, this one is actually three separate photos stitched together in Photoshop.

I’m going to the Living Green Expo in Saint Paul today–it should be a good time. I’m always interested in learning about things that I can do to help the environment. And, best of all, it’s free.
