Rocks in the Lake by Joe Lencioni

July 29, 2007 by Joe Lencioni
Lake Superior, just north of Croftville, MN ƒ/11.0, 15 s, 400 ISO

Similar photos

Sam and I had just finished a 20-mile bike trip from a rest area on the highway to Grand Marais, MN and back.

We pulled in just as the sun was going down. I grabbed my tripod and camera, went down to the water, and snapped off a panorama and a few other shots before it got too dark.
