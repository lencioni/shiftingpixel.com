Moldy Tofu 2

moldy tofu diptych by Joe Lencioni

February 22, 2006 by Joe Lencioni
ƒ/5.6, 2 s, 100 ISO

Similar photos

This is what happens when you leave tofu in the fridge for way way way too long. In case you were wondering, I did this mostly on purpose. You see, when I noticed that it had started to go bad, there were all these different very interesting colors that the mold was making in the tofu. So, I left it to see what would happen. I guess I’m weird.

I was thinking about posting another Holga shot from my tennis bubble session, but I felt like there were a lot of repeats, so I decided to switch it up. Perhaps I’ll scan some more film and rework it a bit to make it more interesting. In the meantime, you’ll have to endure this disgusting diptych.
