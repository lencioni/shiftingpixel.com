This is a simple photo of an iced cappuccino on a table at the River Rock coffee shop here in Saint Peter. I took this photo with the hope that the viewer would be able to easily picture him or herself in the chair, enjoying the nice cold beverage.

One thing that I really like about the River Rock is that they have cool and interesting chairs for you to sit in. Also it’s kind of like an art gallery -there is always something new and exciting to look at on their colorful walls.
