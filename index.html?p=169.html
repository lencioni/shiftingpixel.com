Another sky, another corn field, another night shot. Nothing too exciting. I decided to take a very saturated version of this one. Unfortunately, this one was a little blurry for some reason. I am guessing that I didn’t quite have my focus set correctly–it’s so hard when its way dark out there and I didn’t bring my flashlight so I was using my cellphone for light.
