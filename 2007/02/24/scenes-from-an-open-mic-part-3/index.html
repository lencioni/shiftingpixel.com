This guy does a lot of political satire folk songs. The one he played for us during the open mic focused around the recent Astronaut Lisa Nowak controversy.

There’s a full moon coming up next weekend. I’m getting really excited and crossing my fingers that it’ll be somewhat warm and tolerable weather. Right now it’s not looking too great, but it should be at least above zero. If it’s decent enough, I’m planning on hitting the countryside with my Holga and new cable release apparatus. It’ll be night shots on film for my first time.
