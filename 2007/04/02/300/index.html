Happy April and happy full moon. Unfortunately it has been too cloudy for good night shots lately. I guess I’ll just have to wait for the next one. There’s a blue moon coming soon which means that there’s double the opportunity for night shots. Get out there under the big, bright moon and shoot until you’re tired. Hopefully you’ll be pleasantly surprised with the results.

This is the side door on the historic Methodist church in Cleveland. I’m not entirely sure where it leads inside of the church, but I imagine it going into some sort of a kitchen for some reason. The church may not even have a kitchen for all I know. Definitely my favorite shot from the entire roll.
