Daniel Johnston

I watched The Devil and Daniel Johnston today. It’s a good documentary and definitely worth checking out if you are into indie and underground music. My favorite song of Daniel’s is “Speeding Motorcycle.”

I was also checking out his website and he has a bunch of his art up for sale. Most of it is pretty cool looking, so if you are an art collector, have a look around his store and see if there’s anything that grabs your attention.
