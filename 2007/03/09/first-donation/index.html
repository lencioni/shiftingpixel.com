Although Greased Lightbox has been out for just over a year now, it wasn’t until the other day that I received my first real-life donation for the work that went into the script.

Big thanks to Keith Hale for being thoughtful and generous. Keith, you made my day.

I know that I haven’t updated Greased Lightbox in a while, and it’s starting to age a little bit. I have some plans for it, but other things keep getting in the way. Keep sending your e-mails about it. I am listening, I promise. Things are just taking a little bit longer than I would like them to take.

And, if you find it useful and would like to see it continue to grow, consider donating a little bit. Even $1.00 helps!
