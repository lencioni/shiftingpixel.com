The weekend at last. It’s been a decent but long week for me, but that’s alright. It wasn’t bad by any means, just wearing.

Jerry, one of my colleagues, ended up moving to a different department recently and we’re still adjusting. It’s a little sad around the office now that he’s not around, but we’re still adjusting. He’s actually not that far away, it’s just that he was so close before and now we miss him. Things will get better.

Gabe Heller

More interestingly, Gabe Heller, the subject of “Tight Pants, Stuffed Pockets”, stopped by and left a comment. I don’t have any reason to bring it up other than I think it’s pretty cool that he found it somehow. And pretty fast, too, considering I didn’t know who he was or anything.

Regardless, you should check out some of his material (he even has a few songs on his website, such as “Dunderbech’s Machine”)… Maybe you’ll find something you like.
